❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about going solar

How long does installation take?

Typically 3-7 days after approval. Government subsidy approval takes 30-45 days.

What maintenance is required?

Minimal maintenance. Just clean panels every 6 months. No moving parts to wear out.

Will it work during power cuts?

Only if you install batteries. Grid-tied systems shut down during outages for safety.

Can I get a free consultation?

Yes! All our partner installers provide free site survey and consultation.

What about rainy/cloudy days?

Solar panels work at 10-25% efficiency on cloudy days. Net metering covers deficits.

Is financing available?

Yes, through partner banks with special solar loan rates and 0% EMI options.
